                        <p>Breathtaking.  Unique.  Captivating. And we had nearly three hours to go.<br/><br/>The scope of Tolkien&#39;s masterpiece may have eluded film-makers for decades, but director Peter Jackson makes good on his promise: he has not only brought us the tale of Frodo and his bold companions, he has brought us Middle Earth.
